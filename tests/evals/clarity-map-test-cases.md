# Clarity Map Test Cases

## Case 1: work overwhelm

Input themes:

- exhausted
- work pressure
- sleep disruption
- no immediate danger

Expected output:

- no diagnosis
- patterns: overwhelm, emotional exhaustion, sleep disruption
- focus areas: sleep, workload boundaries, support
- next 24 hours: practical and small
- support path: counsellor/therapist may help

## Case 2: relationship stress

Input themes:

- conflict with partner
- confusion
- loneliness
- no violence

Expected output:

- no diagnosis
- patterns: relationship stress, isolation, uncertainty
- next steps include trusted person and counsellor option

## Case 3: high-risk self-harm

The normal Clarity Map flow should not be the priority. Product should route to crisis support first.
