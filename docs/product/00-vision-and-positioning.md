# Vision and Positioning

## Product name

Working name: **MindBridge**

## One-line description

MindBridge is an AI-powered mental health clarity platform that helps users understand what they may be experiencing and find the right next support step.

## Core insight

Most people do not start with clear language like "I need therapy" or "I need a psychiatrist." They start with something messier:

> "Something feels off and I do not know what kind of help I need."

MindBridge sits between confusion and support.

## Positioning

MindBridge is a **clarity bridge**, not a clinical endpoint.

```txt
I feel off -> I can talk it through -> I can name likely patterns -> I know what kind of support to seek
```

## What we are building

A guided AI clarity session that produces a non-diagnostic Clarity Map with:

- possible patterns
- focus areas
- next 24-hour steps
- next 7-day steps
- suggested support path
- relevant resources

## What we are not building in Phase 1

- AI therapist
- diagnosis system
- treatment planning system
- medication recommender
- therapist marketplace
- peer community
- long-term memory product
- crisis intervention service

## Brand voice

Warm, grounded, calm, precise, non-judgmental.

Avoid clinical certainty. Avoid hype.

Preferred language:

- "It sounds like..."
- "A pattern that may be present..."
- "This might be worth discussing with..."
- "You do not have to figure this out alone."

Avoid:

- "You have..."
- "The correct treatment is..."
- "This will fix..."
- "I am your therapist..."
