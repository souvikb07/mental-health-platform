# Ideal Customer Profile

## Primary ICP for Phase 1

Therapy-curious young adults who feel mentally or emotionally stuck but are not sure what kind of help they need.

## Demographics

- Age: 18-30
- Students, early-career professionals, creators, solo builders
- English-speaking initially
- Comfortable trying AI tools
- Online-first help-seeking behavior

## Psychographics

They may say:

- "I know something is wrong, but I do not know what."
- "I do not know whether this is serious enough for therapy."
- "I do not know how to explain this to someone."
- "I want help, but I do not know where to start."

## Initial wedge

English-speaking young adults in India and global online builder/student/early-career communities.

## Why this ICP works for the hackathon

- Easy to reach online.
- Likely to try an AI product.
- Strong pain around stress, burnout, loneliness, and uncertainty.
- Good fit for public feedback loops.
- Low-friction MVP validation.

## User jobs-to-be-done

Functional job:

> Help me organize what is happening and decide my next support step.

Emotional job:

> Help me feel less alone, less confused, and less ashamed.

Social job:

> Help me explain what is going on to a trusted person or professional.
