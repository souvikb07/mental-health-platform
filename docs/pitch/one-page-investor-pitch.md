# One-Page Investor Pitch

## Product

**MindBridge** helps people who feel mentally or emotionally off understand what they may be experiencing and find the right next support step.

## Problem

People often delay getting help because they cannot name what they are going through or decide what kind of support they need. Existing options are either too generic, too clinical, too expensive, or too intimidating as a first step.

## Solution

MindBridge provides a guided AI clarity session that produces a non-diagnostic Clarity Map: likely patterns, focus areas, immediate steps, 7-day next steps, and relevant support resources.

## Why now

AI makes reflective, personalized support-routing possible at low cost. The gap between vague distress and the right help pathway is large and under-served.

## Phase 1 MVP

- anonymous onboarding
- guided AI conversation
- risk-aware safety routing
- structured Clarity Map
- resource suggestions
- feedback loop

## ICP

Therapy-curious young adults, especially students and early-career professionals, who feel overwhelmed, anxious, numb, burned out, or stuck but are unsure where to start.

## Differentiation

MindBridge does not claim to diagnose or treat. It focuses on clarity and routing: helping users move from confusion to the right kind of human/professional/community support.

## Success metric

User completes a clarity session in under 7 minutes and reports: "I feel clearer about what to do next."
