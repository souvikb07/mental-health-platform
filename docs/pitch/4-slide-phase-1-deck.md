# Phase 1 Four-Slide Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Problem

Title:

> People know something feels wrong. They just cannot name it.

Content:

- Many people feel overwhelmed, anxious, numb, burned out, or stuck.
- They do not know what kind of support they need.
- Search results are generic.
- Therapy feels like a big first step.
- People delay getting help because they cannot explain what is happening.

One-liner:

> The hardest part is the gap between "I think I need help" and "I know what kind of help I need."

## Slide 2: Solution and key features

Title:

> MindBridge: an AI clarity bridge for mental health support.

Key features:

1. Guided AI reflection
2. Safety-aware conversation flow
3. Non-diagnostic Clarity Map
4. Personalized focus areas
5. Next 24-hour and 7-day steps
6. Resource and support routing

Positioning:

> We do not replace therapists. We help people understand what to seek help for.

## Slide 3: Tools and tech stack

Title:

> Built as a fast, safety-aware AI-native web product.

Stack:

- Next.js + TypeScript
- Tailwind + shadcn/ui
- OpenAI API
- Supabase Postgres
- Vercel
- Codex for scaffolding, refactoring, API routes, tests, and review

Architecture:

```txt
Input -> Risk check -> AI response -> Safety validation -> Resource routing
```

## Slide 4: ICP / target audience

Title:

> Built first for therapy-curious young adults who feel stuck but unsure.

ICP:

- Age 18-30
- students, early-career professionals, creators, builders
- comfortable with AI
- hesitant to seek formal mental health support
- wants clarity and a low-friction next step

Positioning line:

> For people who are not ready to say "I need therapy," but are ready to say "I need to understand what is going on."
